About Me

What I do

I'm Júlio Moura, visual artist and creative director at Studio Moosh.

I started with art very early, at age 6, participating in art school projects in my neighborhood in São Paulo.Today, illustration is more than my profession; it’s how I observe, feel, and translate the world. Through editorial illustration and visual storytelling, I aim to create meaningful work that invites reflection and emotion.

At Studio Moosh, each project balances instinct and intention, artistic freedom and narrative purpose.
